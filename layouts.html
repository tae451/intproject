Keyboard Layouts


Above are the most common layouts used in mechanical keyboards. Often, smaller layouts are more popular with FPS gamers who mainly just need WASD. Other types of games, such as MMORPGs, may require more keybinds that necessitate a layout. A numpad included in full-size layouts can be useful for certain types of productivity.

Using multiple layers, it's possible to preserve functionality even with a smaller layout. However, learning how to layers can have a learning curve.

In the end, layouts are a matter of preference, so try each one out and decide which one works for you - there are no limits to what layout you can have with a custom keyboard! Keep in mind a smaller layout will typically be cheaper as well!
